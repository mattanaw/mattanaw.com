Introduction

While it may be possible to read though this website and have some understanding of the total topic, there are some demands on time and intelligence that are insurmountable for the most if not all readers, to come to a thorough understanding of what is being communicated. Articles may be read individually and usefully, and I fully encourage the reader to enjoy the various articles here published. However, this is a very large work. It is similar to a journal, or magazine of many issues, but there is an important difference: it is cohesive and comprehensive, and is intended to be a synthesis of technology and planning. Like a complete academic journal of many issues written over many years, it will not be possible for the reader to fully understand what is shared. Like a large and complex software solution, it cannot be expected that an individual reader can understand it all, or the intentions of the original authors, and architecture and plan, without additional aid and support; and even with that support, it is not anticipated that the primary author or architect’s mind would be known. A piece of technology, of many pieces with distributed interrelated documentation, may not be easily reverse-engineered, and in this case, the author already knows it is impossible. No ready will be able to cover all the material and the technology, and understand all that’s been presented in synthesis.

This website, and document, is one that requires certification if one wants to be able to claim more than a passing reader’s understanding of the material, and the claims that go beyond passing reading are controlled and owned by the author of this site. In other words, no reader may claim to 1) know the mind of the author on the basis of what he here freely published, 2) claim anything other than passing-reader status with respect to what is presented. Additional more specific claims to understanding are only achievable with certification, and are only revealed to serious students or researchers. In this way it is akin to what is encountered in software, since not all that the software comprises and not all of the software sales materials, and meanings of trainings that are sold, are known in advance of serious sales communications. I invite people who have special interest to request certification, and I will readily share what certification will entail for various costs and commitments.

One might consider this a somewhat strange addition to this website; but I assure the reader, this perspective is not only regular and commonplace for all certifications of anything where the reader might make a claim of what the author creates, it is also useful to your life, and your handling of your own legal affairs. Consider that a reader of a publication may be inclined to use that material to claim you think a certain way in court. Using that material in court, they may claim that they understand how you think apart from the article, or how you consider it to fit in with other parts of your work, or life, or goals, or designs. When I mention that a reader or researcher is a passing reader by default, I mean no offense: that is what I am when I read briefly off of someone else’s site, or publication, or if I talk to them for a first time, or share only a few conversations with them. These short communications have not prepared me for any special claim to larger or total knowledge of a person’s mind, plans, designs, goals, or total technology created.

Consider the proposed organizational process below. This was a strategic process option recommendation for a large international corporation for the management of its digital assets. Such a technological process is a group behavior process that would firstly be considered business confidential and proprietary, and secondly, would be corporate intellectual property that is hoped to bring competitive advantages to the business, and add profitability impacting shareholders. Someone from the public would not simply be allowed to learn this process, its significance, its business context, and the so on. This process relates to how jobs are preformed, and the specific tasks that would be carried out by new hires fulfilling specific job functions, partly defined by this and other documents. Within technology, and various business professions, certification is required to show sufficient proficiency for those candidates who may be hired, or those internal candidates who would want to convey later, a transferrable skill. Particularly if the process fits within a software solution that is used elsewhere. Learning about the software would cost money in education expense, and it would be gate-kept information only available to serious candidates or those who spent funds to learn (or had organizations spend funds on their behalf). In this way a certification of a piece of technology relating to behaviors of individuals singly and collectively is normalized in our culture. For this reason it can be understood, again, that a site such as this, which is a piece of technology and a book and journal, would require special school-teaching to arrive at an adequate understanding that would be certifiable. Such teachings protect a business in their ability to control their brand, in that no person who has not attained certification, or adequate teaching, can claim to have mastery. Otherwise anyone from the public could claim to have mastery of software solutions and business professional practices that are used for hiring people who are expected by businesses in the industry to actually have those skills, and some expectations regarding the authority of the cerification that would have been granted by the teaching organization. A person cannot speak on behalf of an organization’s educational content without having obtained and learned that content in a way approved by that organization.

They cannot be trusted to speak about what I would say, do, and think, regarding my own material here, without such a certification. Learnings would be too incomplete due to complexity.

This is a technology solution and not only a writing, but as a writing it is also complex and interrelated, and very large (at present well over 300,000 words), and for this reason it is necessary to protect from pretend experts of the material. I am willing to extend expertise to the reader if the reader is willing to invest in getting certified. However, almost all material is shared already on this site, so I do not want to dissuade the reader from reading freely, from all that has been provided.

This is not a work of religion but does replace religion, and is a religion in a legal sense, in that it replaces all that religion might do, for me. However, there is no superstition, or false promises of things that cannot be provided or are unreal. Religions are not freely available as some might claim, for gaining any level of accepted or authenticated understanding which allows someone to claim expertise. One must attend a theological seminary, or follow other avenues of hierarchical advancement, somewhat akin to exposure in other fields of work, and in education/academia, for finally being accepted or entitled, or given an elevated position of employment, which amount to formal and informal certifications.

This writing is akin to an honest and truthful founding of a religion, creating of a technology for life, an alternative moral system, a technology for mind publication, and for life taxonomy, and much more… It includes software, business processes, intellectual property, personal life processes, academic articles, technology designs, blended in a method of communication, on a software solution entirely created by the author. Legal Limitations

This living autobiography, life, website, book and journal, log, and so on, also has some terms and conditions, and a privacy statement, which incorporates contract law in a way that may not itself be easily understood without explanation and certification. But the author doesn’t rely only on the terms and conditions to explain his behavior and position with respect to the law, including justifications of what here is written and its meaning, and position of what is written in relation to the law. Since this site is a living autobiography, it includes content which is more directly related and resulting from, and evidence of, the author’s behavior and his actual life, that is informed by this work since he actively uses it. This work is the result of his life and is part of his life, and in feedback loops informs his life. His legal justifications of his behavior, or legal rationales for his behavior, where they exist, occur as he thinks about what he will do, and is included within his automatic behavior in his habits.

The terms and conditions and privacy policy, have been artificially separated from life content and other writings on this website. It is important to know that legal content exists in the rest of the sites content. This implies that the terms and conditions, and privacy policy, are not the only legal materials here, and that the entire site must be surveyed in order to locate and understand all the legal positions the author has, that are disclosed and inform his behavior, and might be in his behavior, and in his thinking. The certification program provides additional information which would allow for better interpretation of the author’s legal commitments, access to legal writings, and opportunity to survey all the legal materials which have relevance to understanding the author’s thinking.

Even the certification program, however, is insufficient for conveying to others the author’s legal position in its entirety, and since there is so much legal content to survey, it is unlikely that anyone who obtains certification will have time needed to covney the same information to another. The implication is that certification would be insufficient to provide the person certified wtih witness testimony that is adequate to relay to another person, who has not had a certification, and is uncertified, the knowledge required to understand.

The author thinks it unfortunate that legal contracts are so lengthy and numerous in any individual’s life experience. Legal relationships that are not really possible to “opt out”, meaning they are forced, include also contracts which one must agree to, that are not possible to understand or read thoroughly, such that full understanding is possible. Taken in conjunction, all the legal relationships had provably have legal contracts with legal documents in tens of thousands of pages for any individual. It is not the author’s intention to create contracts with readers or potential students or people wanting certification. The above paragraphs are intended to provide the reader an understanding that the legal position of the author requires even more than this unfortunately, for obtaining a full knowledge of the author’s legal position, outside of having contracts with the author. Any particular business may require one to have a legal contract, signed, for a service that is really necessary, like cellular phone communication or internet connectivity, which may have many thousand words of text written by trained lawers coming from a law school. Mattanaw, who is a pro-se attorney and experienced litigant, has done more to explain his own legal position, by interspersing legal information in his website within his autobiography, this Book and Journal, numbering in the thousands of pages, and hundreds of thousands of words. Soon there will be over a million words, with legal material provided, in context for its proper interpretation, within Mattanaw’s life and life recordings.

Mattanaw’s legal position can only be understood within the context of his life, and so it is natural that legal documents and content expressing his legal position would appear in context for interpretation to be possible. The entire Book and Journal is an evidence submission, or exhibit submission, which would need to be completely read by a court system, to obtain a level of understanding which is required, but not good enough for certification. Legal reading is not a substitute for certification, which requires payment. This would provide additional information as to the value of certification, for providing more training than what is obtainable by a court system or judge. In a way this resembles all education. A court system cannot understand science, for not having been educated scientifically in a university setting. Likewise, a complex life cannot be understood, without dedicated study of that life.
